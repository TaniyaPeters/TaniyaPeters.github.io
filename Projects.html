Project: Thales Arduino Competition
Complete: Yes

The Thales Arduino Competition was hosted by Thales from October 10th to October 31st, 2017. I had applied earlier that year for the Thales Arduino Competition and was one of twenty four students who were selected. All twenty fout students were split into 6 teams of four people. My group consisted of two 4th year electrical engingeerg students, a computer science and me.
The Thales Competition had a few rules:

  • An Arduino must be used
  • The project had to be inspired by Thales
  • Use of other equipment was limited and need to be asked permission, e.g. Raspberry Pi
